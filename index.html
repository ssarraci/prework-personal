BTS is a seven-member South Korean boy band that debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The genre of music they produce ranges from ballad to EDM and everything in between. Recently in 2020 they even produced a song that was completely in english, Dynamite. Not only does BTS create amazing music but they also talk about self love and incorporate it in their music.
